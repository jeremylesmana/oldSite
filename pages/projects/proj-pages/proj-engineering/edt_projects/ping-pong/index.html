Ping Pong Launcher Project

          The current assigned project involves students to brainstorm, design, and invent a machine that can complete a specified mission or task. The students will work in pairs of two, competing against other classes and their own classmates as well. The task assigned was to create a machine/contraption that can launch a ping pong ball into a 5 gallon bucket from 10 feet away. The materials provided will be foam board, wooden sticks, glue, and approved materials by the instructor. The one and only allowed power source are rubber bands. The mechanism must have a triggering system and must have a limit to specific dimensions before triggered.
