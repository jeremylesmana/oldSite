Bridge Building Project

          The Engineering Design Technology Bridge Design project was designed to form theories and increase construction skills competitively against classmates and peers. Students will be required to design a model of a bridge both physically and in software such as SolidWorks and the Bridge Designer that can withstand the most load (in this case weights) and also be efficient enough to require the least material possible. Students also have to try to achieve the right amount of tension and compression, in order for the bridge to work efficiently. The bridges must try to obtain the maximum strength possible in order to receive the smallest possibility of failure.
