WHAT IS HONEYBOBA?

Honeyboba is a Southern California based boba shop that serves various types of drinks with numerous toppings. Our most famous topping would be the honey boba, which are tapioca pearls that are submerged into honey which gives it the tremendous flavor in your tastebuds! Don't miss out on our wonderful toppings such as the honey boba, egg pudding, taro pudding, coffee jelly, lychee jelly, grass jelly, red bean, rainbow jelly, aloe vera, and more. Not only that we provide you different options of drinks with multiple toppings to choose from, but you are also allowed to choose your own sweetness scale of your drink for those of you with the sweet tooth! Feel free to browse around our site, where you can find the menu, our locations, contacts, and information on hiring!
